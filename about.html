About Rational Horizon Academy

A Father's Vision

Rational Horizon Academy was created by Richard Anaya, a father driven by the desire to provide his daughter with an education that would cultivate her capacity for independent thought and passionate pursuit of her own values. Leveraging the power of artificial intelligence, this curriculum was developed to embody the philosophical principles he discovered through Ayn Rand's work.

This represents more than an educational program—it's a deeply personal commitment to nurturing reason, individualism, and the pursuit of rational happiness. Grounded in Objectivist principles, the curriculum aims to develop young minds that think critically, value truth above conformity, and embrace their unlimited potential.

Our Mission

We seek to provide children with the intellectual foundation they need to become confident, rational individuals who understand their own worth and capability. Every lesson is designed to instill the critical thinking skills and philosophical clarity necessary for a lifetime of learning, achievement, and genuine happiness.
